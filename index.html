🏰 Disney World

Distance from hotel: ~20 minutes drive • Transport: Uber/Lyft recommended (parking is expensive)

🎟️ Disney World Ticket (Simple Explanation)

Disney World has different ticket types based on how many parks you want to visit in a day. You must pick the ticket type when you buy it.

🎢 1-Park Per Day Ticket (Most common & simple)

  • Visit only ONE Disney park per day
  • You must choose which park you will visit that day
  • On another day, you can visit a different park

📍 Disney has 4 main parks

  • Magic Kingdom
  • EPCOT
  • Disney’s Hollywood Studios
  • Disney’s Animal Kingdom
👉 Example: If you buy a ticket for Magic Kingdom, you can enter only Magic Kingdom that day (not EPCOT or other parks).

✅ Best Disney Park for our group

Magic Kingdom – BEST OVERALL CHOICE

  • Best mix of toddler rides + older kids + adults
  • Many rides with no height restriction
  • Characters, parades, fireworks
  • Evening shows after 6:00 PM

Good rides for all ages

  • It’s a Small World (2–4 yrs)
  • Peter Pan
  • Dumbo
  • Pirates of the Caribbean
  • Buzz Lightyear
  • Seven Dwarfs Mine Train (7+ yrs)
  • Parade & fireworks (everyone ❤️)

Skipping (this trip)

  • EPCOT – more adult-focused, fewer rides for toddlers
  • Hollywood Studios – better for 8+ kids, more thrill rides
👉 Plan: Decide the Disney park first, then buy the ticket only for that park/day.

🎢 Universal Orlando

Distance from hotel: ~30 minutes drive

🎟️ Universal Tickets (Simple Explanation)

Universal tickets are priced based on how many parks you visit in one day.

🎢 1-Park, 1-Day Ticket (Cheaper)

  • Visit only ONE park in a day
  • Choose either: Universal Studios Florida OR Islands of Adventure
  • You cannot enter the other park the same day

🎢 2-Park, 1-Day Ticket (More expensive)

  • Visit BOTH parks in the same day (Universal Studios + Islands)
  • Includes the Harry Potter train between the parks

📍 Universal parks list

  • Universal Studios Florida
  • Islands of Adventure
  • Epic Universe (separate ticket)
  • Volcano Bay (water park)

✅ Best Universal park for our group

Islands of Adventure – BEST CHOICE

  • Dr. Seuss area for toddlers
  • Fun rides for 7–10 yr kids
  • Thrill rides for adults

Skipping (this trip)

  • Universal Studios Florida – fewer toddler rides
  • Epic Universe – mostly thrill-based
  • Volcano Bay – tiring for small kids
👉 Plan: Choose one Universal park per day and buy tickets only for that park/day.

🐬 SeaWorld Orlando

Distance from hotel: ~30 minutes drive

🎟️ Ticket

  • Single park ticket

Best for

  • Animal experiences (shows, aquariums)
  • Bigger kids (7+ years)
  • Adults who enjoy rides + animals
📌 Note: Fewer rides for toddlers. Good option for a lighter park day.

🧱 LEGOLAND Florida

Distance from hotel: ~1 hour drive

🎟️ Ticket

  • One park ticket (all rides in one park)

Best for

  • Toddlers and young kids (2–7 years)
  • Relaxed, colorful, kid-friendly environment
  • Shorter lines and less walking
✅ Why we like it: All rides are kids-focused and it’s a great rest day between big parks.

🚀 NASA – Kennedy Space Center

Distance from hotel: ~2 hours drive

🎟️ Ticket

  • Single-day admission

Best for

  • Educational experience
  • Kids 7+ years and adults
📌 Note: Long drive. Best planned as a full-day trip. Optional depending on energy level.
✅ Final Important Note (For Everyone):
For Disney and Universal, we will decide the park first, buy tickets only for the selected park/day, and avoid multi-park tickets unless needed.
